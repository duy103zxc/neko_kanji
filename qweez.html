Welcome to the Neko Kanji Quiz, where you can test your knowledge of kanji characters and their readings. This quiz offers a challenging yet enjoyable experience to enhance your understanding of Kanji.

How to Play:

  • Choose between Regular Mode or Time Attack Mode.
  • In Regular Mode, set the desired number of questions for a customized experience.
  • In Time Attack Mode, challenge yourself with a set time limit of your choice.
  • Enable the hint option for additional assistance.
  • Write the Kanji reading in either hiragana or romaji.
  • Press Enter to submit your answer.
  • You have three chances per question to get it right.
  • If you want to end the quiz prematurely, you can type "end" in the input field.
  • Have fun!
